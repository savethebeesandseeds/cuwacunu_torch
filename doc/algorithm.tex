\begin{abstract}
This manuscript lays the mathematical foundation for \Cuwacunu{}, an artificial
intelligence agent for portfolio management.  The system reads
multi-resolution market data as edge-carried signals on an active market graph,
learns representations of traded relations, emits predictive distribution
fields, and lifts selected edge forecasts back into node-level portfolio
states.  The current draft fixes the tensor, graph, distribution, and observer
objects needed to connect implementation, experiments, and analysis.  Its
purpose is to make the forecasting interface precise while preserving the larger
direction: portfolio positioning under abstract values.
\end{abstract}
